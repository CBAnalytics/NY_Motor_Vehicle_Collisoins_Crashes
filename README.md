# NY_Motor_Vehicle_Collisoins_Crashes

A comprehensive data analyst project focusing on vehicle collision data across New York City. 

## Project Overview

This project analyzes NYC vehicle collision data to provide insights for improving driver and pedestrian safety across New York boroughs and to determine the effectiveness of current policies. 

## Business Problems

- Change in traffic accidents across NY with current policies.
- Traffic accident hot spots.
- Leading causes of traffic accidents.
- Groups that face the highest risk of injury or death.

- Tableau
